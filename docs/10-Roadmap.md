# Development Roadmap

# LearnOS

Version: v0.1 Beta

---

# Purpose

This roadmap defines the development phases of LearnOS.

The objective is to build LearnOS incrementally while ensuring every version is usable, scalable, and production-ready.

Every phase should deliver a complete experience rather than incomplete features.

---

# Product Vision

LearnOS is not a course platform.

It is an AI Operating System for Learning Anything.

Every release should move closer to this vision.

# Long-Term Mission

LearnOS aims to become the world's first AI Operating System for Learning Anything.

The platform will understand every learner, adapt continuously, and guide people from curiosity to real-world outcomes.

Every version of LearnOS should move closer to this mission.

---

# Development Principles

- Build one complete experience at a time.
- Prioritize quality over quantity.
- Every feature must support personalization.
- AI should make decisions, not just generate text.
- Keep the architecture modular.
- Design for lifelong learning.
- Build with startup quality from day one.
